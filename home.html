How to keep your pets occupied during the coronavirus lockdown.

The current restrictions, such as physical distancing and lockdown, due to the COVID-19 pandemic has an impact on both humans and animals. While this can be an anxious time for everybody it is important to see things positively and use this as an opportunity to do things, we might not usually have time for.
